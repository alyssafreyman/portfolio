About Me

I've always had a passion for creating innovative projects, whether through graphic design, videography, or UX/UI design. With a BA in Communications (Filmmaking) and Creative Technologies from Berry College, I've had the privilege of working on a wide range of exciting projects since then. Currently, I'm earning my Masters in Computer Science from the Georgia Institute of Technology.

Having worked across various aspects of communication and technology, I feel incredibly fortunate to do what I love every day. From capturing stunning visuals to meeting people from all walks of life, I’m constantly learning and growing in my craft. I’m always eager for the next challenge, so if you have an idea and are looking to collaborate, I'd love to hear from you.
