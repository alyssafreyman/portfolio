— Video & Audio / Fictional Series

Reality
Check

Reality Check is a 4-episode limited series that was made through the Viking Fusion production team. I was the screenwriter, director, and director of photography for the show. It was filmed during the fall semester of 2021 and was released in the spring semester of 2022.

Role Director, Screenwriter, Editor
Format Fictional Series
Episodes 4 Episodes
Platform YouTube

Watch the First Episode

— How It Was Made

Process

This series all started with a script that I wrote. I separated the script into four mini episodes. I got feedback from friends and mentors on my writing. I conducted auditions, cast the roles and hired crew, and I set up filming schedules.

I was the Director on set. I made sure that we were on schedule and that everything aligned with the script.

I edited the footage using Adobe Premiere Pro, Audition, and After Effects. I gathered feedback from my team and made some changes before the premiere. My producer and I planned a premiere at my college to help advertise the series.
